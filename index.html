The Solution

What is OPI?

OPI — Other People's Insurance — is the strategy of using the seller's existing homeowner's insurance policy to fund storm damage repairs before you ever close the deal.

When hail hits a property, the insurance company owes for the damage. The deductible is typically just 1% of the home's value. The rest of the claim — roof, gutters, AC, fencing, windows — gets paid out.

Most investors don't know this. You're about to.
