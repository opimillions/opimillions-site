The 5-Point Check — What You'll Know How to Do

Each point on this checklist represents money most investors walk right past.

1

Check Gutters & Downspouts

Bowl-shaped dents reveal hail size and storm intensity. One of the clearest indicators adjusters look for first.

2

Inspect Window Screens

Hail punches distinctive holes in screens. Often worth $800+ on a claim — and almost always missed by investors.

3

Check the AC Condenser

Hail flattens AC fins. A damaged condenser can add $2,850 or more to an insurance claim — worth documenting every time.

4

Look for Granules in Gutters

When hail hits shingles, granules knock off into the gutters. Full gutters = damaged roof. No ladder required.

5

Check Roof Vents & Flashings

Metal vents show hail impacts clearly. Spatter marks on soft metals reveal exact hail size and storm direction.
