Investor's Hail Hit Checklist

Quick 5-point field check · Takes less than 2 minutes

Before walking a flip property, run this quick check. 3 or more hits = high claim potential. Pull the dec page and dig deeper before making your offer.

1
Dented Gutters & Downspouts
Look for bowl-shaped dents. Use flashlight parallel to surface to cast shadows. Drag chalk to highlight. Hail dents are sporadic, not linear.
2
Damaged Window Screens
Small tears, holes, or dents in screens. Easy to miss — use flashlight to illuminate. Often overlooked by adjusters but adds to claim total.
3
HVAC Fins / Coils Impacted
Check AC condenser for bent fins and spatter marks (oxidation knocked off = dark spots). Bent fins restrict airflow. Worth $2,850+ in replacement value.
4
Granule Loss in Gutters
Check gutter pan for accumulated granules from shingle impacts. If gutters are full of granules, the roof took a hit. Ladder needed for best view.
5
Dents in Flashing or Roof Vents
Metal roof vents, turbines, and flashings show hail impacts clearly. Use chalk to highlight. Spatter marks reveal hail size and direction of storm.
Quick Score
4–5 ✓
Strong — pull dec page now
2–3 ✓
Possible — verify storm date
0–1 ✓
Low — note and move on
